FAQ

Important details before starting

These notes help set expectations and avoid confusion before a project starts.

Does the ₱5,000 package include everything?

No. It is a starter setup for basic needs. Larger websites, custom graphics, payment setup, automation, and app features require a higher quote.

Is Maya approval guaranteed?

No. Maya approval depends on Maya's own requirements. We can help make your website and payment flow presentation ready, but Maya decides approval.

Are domain, hosting, ads, and third-party fees included?

Not unless stated in the proposal. Government fees, domain fees, hosting fees, ad budget, and platform charges are usually separate.

Can I request a custom budget?

Yes. Tell us your available budget and we will recommend the most realistic deliverables for that amount.
