FAQ

Important details before a project starts

These answers set expectations early and reduce confusion around pricing, scope, payment support, and project delivery.

Does ₱5,000 include everything?

No. It is a starter package designed for simple website needs. Additional pages, custom visuals, payment setup, automation, and broader scope require a higher quotation.

Is Maya approval guaranteed?

No. Intellium Digital can prepare the website and customer flow to be payment-ready, but final approval still depends on Maya or the chosen payment provider.

Are domain, hosting, and ads included?

Only if they are clearly included in the approved proposal. Domain fees, hosting fees, and ad budgets are usually billed separately.

Can I request a custom budget?

Yes. Share your available budget and business goals, and we will recommend the strongest possible scope within that range.

How long does a project take?

Timeline depends on the scope, content readiness, revisions, and integrations. Simpler landing pages move faster than larger websites or app builds.
